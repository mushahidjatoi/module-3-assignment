chicken

A chicken dish is a meal or recipe that primarily features chicken as the main ingredient. Chicken dishes can take many forms, such as roasted chicken, chicken curry, chicken stir-fry, chicken soup, or even chicken sandwiches. Essentially, it's any food preparation that centers around chicken, which is a popular and versatile meat used in various culinary creations worldwide.

Sushi

Sushi is a popular Japanese dish made from seasoned rice with fish, egg, or vegetables. A sushi roll is shaped inside a thin sheet of seaweed. Sushi comes from a Japanese word meaning "sour rice," and it's the rice that's at the heart of sushi, even though most Americans think of it as raw fish.
